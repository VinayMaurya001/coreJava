The Problem
It was formulated by Dijkshtra in 1965.
At a table:
	5 Philosopher
	5 Chopstick/Fork
Philosopher can:
	Eat
	Think
Philosopher can eat when they have both left & right chopsticks.
A chopstick can be hold by one Philosopher at a given time.
The Problem:How ot create a concurrent algorithm such that no Philosopher will starve.
